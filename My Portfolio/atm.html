ATM

Details of Project

An automated teller machine(ATM)is an electronic telecommunications device that enables customers of financial institutions to perform financial transaction, such as cash withdrawals, deposits or obtanaining account information, at any time and without the need for direct interaction with bank staff.

The ATM must be able to provide the following service to the customer

We can check the account balance, withdraw amount from the personal bank account and deposit money and print receipt

The ATM will service one customer at a time. A customer will be required to insert an ATM card and enter a personal identification number (PIN) .if the bank determines the customers pin is invalid, the customer will be required to re-enter the pin before a transaction can process.
